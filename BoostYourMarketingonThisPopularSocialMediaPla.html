Boost Your Marketing on This Popular Social Media Platform

Social media marketing is the way of the future. There’s no need to worry about television or radio advertisements anymore. As long as you can get word of your small business onto your target audience’s feed, you’re going to bring in lots of customers. And if you want to cast your net into a wide pool, the social media platform that you have to hit is Facebook.

How Should You Do It?

Get Funding

The first thing that you should do is get access to funding. Create a Business Page

The next thing that you should do is make a Facebook business page to post content and engage with followers. It’s your official home base on the social media platform. Start by creating a page and then fleshing out all of the crucial info:

  • Your brand name
  • Your business address
  • Your website
  • Your other social media accounts
  • A summary of your business purpose/goals
  • Photos of your products, business and staff

Divulge as much information as you can in there. You want people to learn as much about you as possible.

Set Up Ads

Now, if you don’t already have a large customer base or dedicated social media following, it will take a while to bring in followers. To speed things up, you should think about setting up targeted Facebook ads to get your ideal customers to see your brand. The move could get them to follow your page. It could get them directly to your website. And it could get them to buy your products/services after a few clicks.

Why Facebook?

Social media apps come and go, so why should you place your bets on Facebook? Simply put, it’s the biggest one. When you look at the statistics of the most popular social media networks in the entire world, Facebook comes out on top.

On a smaller scale, seven in ten adults in the country use the platform. And more than half of those adults visit the platform every single day. Starting your marketing campaign on there is a quick way to reach a massive and dedicated audience that’s looking for the next big thing on their feed.

If you’re going to choose a social media network to market on, you should choose Facebook. It’s the biggest for a reason. Once you manage to bring in customers on this gigantic site, every other network will feel like a piece of cake.
